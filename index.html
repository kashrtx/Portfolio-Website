About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Kaushal Bhingaradia, a student at the University of Toronto, and a passionate software developer. In my TCS major at University of Toronto, we are exposed to a lot of UI/UX design concepts such as user-centric design and accessibility. This is where we make various projects that showcase our understanding of these concepts through responsive websites, software, Unity games, and UI-related projects. Courses like CCT286 and CCT270 have taught me a great deal about creating accessible and user-centric content. I am also majoring in GIS, which has introduced me to more advanced concepts like spatial data analysis, querying with SQL, and Python scripting. This knowledge allows me to build on my skills and create even more advanced projects in the future. I am very creative and love exploring new things to develop various projects.
